The Founder's Story

"I founded Lumière with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where time stands still, and every meal is a masterpiece."

Starting in a small kitchen in Paris, Chef Antoine's journey has been defined by an unyielding pursuit of excellence. He believes that true luxury lies in the details—the texture of the bread, the warmth of the wine, and the genuine connection between host and guest.

Today, Lumière stands as a testament to that original dream, blending French tradition with modern innovation.

Antoine Dubois
